c. Fraud, Misrepresentation, and Other Findings i. Standard The nonprofit must take prompt, effective, and corrective measures to investigate and document suspected instances of fraud, misrepresentation, and other related Findings. ii. Internal Reporting to Senior Management The nonprofit’s QC Plan must contain a process for its QC staff to promptly report and document Findings delivered to senior management. Nonprofit staff must report Findings to senior management no more than 15 business days from the date of discovery. iii. External Reporting to FHA The nonprofit’s senior management must contact the Program Support Division at the Jurisdictional Homeownership Center (HOC) to submit QC Findings. HUD will review the Findings and determine the appropriate course of action.
