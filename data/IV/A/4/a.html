a. Withdrawal of Application for Insurance Benefits If the claim has not yet been paid, the Mortgagee may apply in writing to HUD’s MCM for consent to withdraw an application of insurance benefits. The Mortgagee must agree to: • accept Reconveyance of the Property; • promptly file a Reconveyance for record; • accept the title evidence it furnished to HUD; and • reimburse HUD for expenses incurred in holding the Property.
